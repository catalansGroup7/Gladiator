Home Loan EMI Calculator

Buying a house is not only a dream, but a great financial investment. At such time a Home loan is of great help and also a wise choice. A loan provides financial resource. The Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) setting helps to ease out a sudden financial load. Distributing your expense over time. Making repayment comfortable.
Well, but before making an investment, it is always a wise choice to assess how much EMI has to be payed. As it is an important financial decision.
The EMI consists of two parts. The Principal amount and the Interest generated on the Home Loan. So, the EMI is equal to the sum of the principal amount and the interest to be paid on the loan.
The Fixed EMI is required to be repaid over the given tenure on monthly basis. EMIs are calculated over reducing balance method. Which means initially the EMIs will pay more towards interest. Later on it repays the principal component, this is an advantage to the borrower, which is you. Thus making it a wise financial decision.
The interest and tenure you choose are key factors to your EMIs.
